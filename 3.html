What do I do?

I do a lot of things. This is another thing that I really enjoy being in Argentina.
For me, the life style in Argentina is totally different from where I was born and raised.
I am from Seoul, where everything works so fast. In Seoul, you need to think fast, act fast, and get the result fast.
But in Argentina, it is completely opposite.

I work 8 hours from Monday to Friday. I also enjoy cooking.
I finally understood how to enjoy the slow process of cooking something, and taste my own work.
I am into the Wire craft art. I make my own earrings and rings.
I also have new passion, which is now coding, learning HTML, CSS, Java Script, Python.
I try to take some part of Seoul off, and to learn how to live my life fully.
I seek for what I like, what I would like to try. And try to enjoy every moment. That is what I do.
